The Category

Why we call it 4D Sales Intelligence.

Most CI tools work in one or two dimensions. Dashboards show what happened. Alerts fire when a competitor moves. That is flat. Real competitive intelligence requires four dimensions — and synthesis across all of them.

D1 · WIDTH

Width.

Every call. Every deal.

Most teams listen to 10 or 20 calls per quarter. We analyze 500 or more. Statistical patterns from your entire competitive deal flow — not anecdotes from a cherry-picked sample. The objections that keep killing deals are visible only when the sample is your whole library.

D2 · DEPTH

Depth.

Synthesized across sources.

Sales calls, CRM deal data, win/loss interviews, prospect emails, post-mortem feedback — correlated as one intelligence layer. What buyers told you, what reps said, what the CRM shows, what closed and why. Single-source CI is half the story.

D3 · TIME

Time.

Patterns over quarters.

Point-in-time alerts are noise without context. We track competitive movement across quarters: pricing shifts, positioning drift, objection evolution, win-rate trajectory by competitor. The story your data tells is in the trend, not the snapshot.

D4 · PERSPECTIVE

Perspective.

Buyer's voice and seller's voice.

Most platforms hear only one side. We capture both: what your buyers actually said in calls and emails, and what your reps positioned, conceded, and asked. The truth lives in the gap between them — and that gap is where the deals you should have won went sideways.
