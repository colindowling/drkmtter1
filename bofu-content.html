The Problem

Your buyers are at the bottom of the funnel.
Your content is at the top.

The average B2B buying decision involves 6 to 10 searches. Most happen after the buyer already knows they have a problem — they're evaluating vendors, comparing pricing, researching alternatives.

These are BoFu searches — Bottom of Funnel. The highest-intent, highest-converting searches in your market. And most content programs completely ignore them.

Meanwhile, your buyers are typing "[competitor] alternative," "[competitor] pricing," and "best [your category] for [their industry]" — and landing on someone else's page.
