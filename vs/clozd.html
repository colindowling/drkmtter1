Where DARK MATTER goes further.

Every deal, not a sample

Clozd interviews 20-30 buyers per quarter. DARK MATTER analyzes every competitive call your team recorded. The patterns from 500 calls are statistically robust in ways a 25-person sample can't match.

Continuous, not periodic

Win/loss interviews produce quarterly snapshots. DARK MATTER delivers weekly intelligence updates. When the competitive landscape shifts, you know in days, not months.

Actionable artifacts, not reports

Clozd delivers research reports. DARK MATTER delivers battlecards reps use tomorrow, talk tracks proven to win this quarter, and threat assessments that drive strategy sessions.

Real-time buyer language

Clozd captures what buyers say after the decision is made. DARK MATTER captures what they say during the deal. The signal is different and often more honest during the conversation than in retrospective interviews.
