Competitive Landscape

How the market tries to solve this today.

Three categories. All leave the hardest part — synthesis — to you.

CI Platforms

Klue · Crayon · Kompyte

What they do

Track external competitor signals — website changes, press releases, pricing pages, review sentiment, hiring patterns.

Who owns it

Requires a dedicated PMM or CI analyst to curate, maintain battlecards, and distribute.

Blind spot

Never touches your actual sales conversations. No call analysis. No buyer voice from your pipeline.

Win-Loss Interview Firms

Clozd · Thirdside · Trinity

What they do

Conduct 10–30 third-party buyer interviews per year. Transcribe, summarize, tag keywords, and post to a portal.

Who owns it

Managed service — but each interview is a standalone artifact. Client must read and synthesize them.

Blind spot

No cross-interview pattern tracking. No correlation with CRM data or rep call behavior. No longitudinal trends.

Conversation Intelligence

Gong · Chorus · Clari

What they do

Record and transcribe your sales calls. Surface keywords, competitor mentions, and basic AI summaries.

Who owns it

Self-serve — someone on your team has to query, filter, listen, and extract the intelligence manually.

Blind spot

Great at capture. Zero synthesis. No battlecard generation, no board narrative, no competitive strategy output.
